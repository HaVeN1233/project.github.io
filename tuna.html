TUNA PASTEL

Home Information

This presentation, titled "Tuna Pastel: A Culinary Delight & Fisheries Connection," aims to educate, particularly fisheries students, about the intersection of culinary practices and sustainable seafood sourcing. It uses the popular dish, Tuna Pastel, as a springboard to discuss responsible consumption and the challenges facing the tuna fishing industry.


The presentation is structured to be easily understood, employing simple language and impactful visuals. It covers key aspects of Tuna Pastel preparation, emphasizing the importance of using sustainably sourced tuna. This is achieved by encouraging the identification of certifications like the Marine Stewardship Council (MSC) label, which guarantees responsible fishing practices.


Beyond the recipe, the presentation delves into broader issues within the tuna fishing industry, including overfishing, bycatch, and illegal fishing activities. It highlights the crucial role of scientific research and effective management strategies in ensuring the long-term health of tuna populations and the sustainability of the industry. The overall message promotes a connection between culinary enjoyment and environmental responsibility, urging viewers to make informed choices that support both their palates and the health of the oceans.
